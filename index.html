About

Studio Anjou is a solo digital artwork studio run by Angela Fernihough. With more than 15 years of experience in artwork production and prepress, I specialise in refining, adapting and preparing artwork for print and digital use.

I work extensively with Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator to retouch, update, recolour and prepare artwork to professional production standards.

Working remotely from the UK, I support agencies, printers and businesses with reliable artwork services including artwork updates, retouching, recolouring and batch artwork processing. My focus is always on delivering accurate, production-ready files that meet technical specifications.

Bilingual communication available in English and French.

Services

Print Artwork Preparation

Preparing files for professional print including bleed setup, colour conversion and press-ready PDFs.

Artwork Adaptation

Updating and reworking existing artwork for new formats, campaigns, languages or product variations.

Retouching

Cleaning, refining and enhancing artwork using Adobe Photoshop.

Recolouring

Updating artwork colours to match brand guidelines or Pantone specifications.

Batch Artwork Processing

Preparing large volumes of artwork files efficiently and consistently.

Vector Artwork

Redrawing and preparing vector artwork using Adobe Illustrator.

Instructional & Educational Graphics

Creation of clear diagrams and visuals for manuals, guides and training materials.

Ongoing Artwork Support

Reliable artwork production support for agencies and businesses requiring regular updates or campaign rollouts.
