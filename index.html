Artwork Production & Digital Artworking Support

Studio Anjou is the freelance digital artwork studio of Angela Fernihough, a UK-based artworker with over 15 years of experience supporting design studios, publishers and marketing teams.

I specialise in preparing, refining and updating artwork for print and digital use — helping creative teams deliver accurate, production-ready files.

Based in the UK and working remotely, I collaborate with clients nationally and internationally.

Services

  • Artwork production and artwork updates
  • Preparing files for print and digital publication
  • Artwork corrections and amends
  • Image retouching and clean-up
  • Recolouring graphics and illustrations
  • Batch artwork file preparation
  • Versioning and localisation of artwork
  • Converting artwork between formats
  • Rebuilding or refining existing artwork
  • Simple website graphics and interface elements
